Attention: Epilepsy Warning Disclaimer
Before using this game, please consider the following:
This game contains bright circles and flashes that may trigger seizures for people with photosensitive epilepsy. If you or anyone in your family has a history of seizures or photosensitive reactions, please consult with your doctor before using this game.
If you experience dizziness, see colored spots, suffer from muscle twitches, lose consciousness, or notice any other unusual symptoms, immediately stop using the game and consult a doctor.
To reduce the risk of epileptic seizures, it is recommended to play in a well-lit room and take breaks every 30-60 minutes.
